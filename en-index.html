A patented amino acid blend. An ingredient that shapes the taste.

Proamina® is a patented food ingredient, developed by NH2 Tech Srl in Milan, and distributed exclusively across Italy and the European Union by Italprotein.

It is not a food additive, not a Novel Food, not a polyol. It is an amino acid blend classified as an ordinary food ingredient under Regulation (EC) No 178/2002. Among its properties, it contributes positively to the aromatic profile of the finished product and allows the producer to reduce the amount of added sugar in the formulation.

Features

Technical Properties

Proamina® is a patented amino acid blend classified as an ordinary food ingredient (Reg. EC 178/2002). It comes as white crystals, freely soluble in water, and contributes positively to the aromatic profile of the finished product.

Unlike additives and polyols, it is a 100% protein ingredient with zero carbohydrates, zero fat and zero sugars, delivering quality amino acids with an intrinsically positive nutritional profile.

💧

Freely soluble

White crystals that dissolve quickly in water (280.9 g/L) and in any drink, hot or cold.

🧬

100% protein profile

Zero carbohydrates, zero fat, zero sugars. Contributes to the protein content of the finished product.

🏆

Stable in baking

Thermostable up to 214 °C. Full functionality at pH 5.5-6.5 and in acidic environments typical of fermented dairy products.

📋

Clean label

Ordinary food ingredient, not an additive, no E-number, no "with sweetener" declaration, no laxative warnings.

The magic of the cappuccino.

The protein nature of Proamina® interacts with milk proteins during foaming, forming an enhanced network: a denser, smoother, more persistent microfoam.

The result is a cappuccino with a cream that brings out the barista's latte art, a full taste profile and zero carbohydrates. Works with any kind of milk.
