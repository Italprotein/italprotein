The science

How Proamina® works

Proamina® is a pure amino acid blend obtained through a patented process. The amino acids activate the T1R2/T1R3 sweet receptors as a sugar replacement, with no carbohydrate intake and without contributing to the formation of AGEs (Advanced Glycation End-products).

⚗️

Origin: patented blend

Amino acid blend produced through a patented process. White crystals, freely soluble in water (280.9 g/l), pH 5.85.

🧬

Pure protein metabolism

Metabolised like any amino acid. Zero carbohydrates, zero fats. Compatible with ketogenic diet, low-carb regimes and protein-forward protocols.

🧪

Technological behaviour in food matrices

The protein nature of Proamina® interacts with food matrices opening up new formulation possibilities, from beverages to baked goods to industrial pastry.

🏆

Patented blend, stable in cooking

Stable in cooking and baking. Patented in Italy with international PCT extension. Proamina® brand registered internationally.
